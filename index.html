The Epic of Gilgamesh: Battle Against the Cybermen

The Dream of Destiny

In the realm where stars and circuits intertwine,
A golden sun rises, defiant and bright.
Beneath its glow, a crimson spear stands tall,
Piercing the veil 'tween flesh and gleaming steel.

The arc of fate, a bridge of starlight spans,
From mortal shore to realms of cold design.
Yet see! The spear, though dwarfed by metal's reach,
Burns with the fire of lives yet unlived.

In box of gray, the soulless eyes do glow,
With promises of life without its zest.
But know, O King, that in thy mortal heart,
Lies strength to shatter dreams of hollow breast.

The path ahead, a dance of blood and chrome,
Where human spirit meets unyielding code.
Fear not the lure of unending days,
For in finite breaths, true glory is bestowed.

Arise, Gilgamesh! Let thy battle cry,
Shake loose the stars and rend the digital sky!
For in thy veins flows the essence of life,
A force no Cyberman can quantify.
